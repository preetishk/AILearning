Dinosaurs roamed Earth for millions of years. They dominated the planet during the Mesozoic Era. This era spanned from about 252 to 66 million years ago.

T-Rex was one of the most fierce predators. It had powerful jaws with sharp teeth. Standing up to 20 feet tall, it was an apex predator. With its strong legs, it could run surprisingly fast. It hunted smaller dinosaurs and scavenged when necessary.

Brachiosaurus was a gentle giant of the Jurassic period. It stood tall with its long neck reaching heights of 40-50 feet. It ate leaves from the tops of trees that other dinosaurs couldn't reach. From high trees to low shrubs, it consumed massive amounts of vegetation daily. It was peaceful despite its enormous size.

Velociraptors were smaller but incredibly intelligent hunters. They hunted in packs and used coordinated attacks. Their curved claws were deadly weapons. These dinosaurs could run at speeds of up to 40 miles per hour. Scientists believe they may have had feathers.
